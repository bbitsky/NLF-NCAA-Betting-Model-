🏈 NFL Key Predictive Factors

EPA per Play
Best single predictor. Measures scoring value of every play beyond down/distance expectation.
DVOA
Football Outsiders' Defense-adjusted Value Over Average. Context-adjusts for opponent quality.
PFF Grades
Pro Football Focus unit grades (OL, secondary, pass rush) — critical for props & totals.
Line Movement / Sharp $
Steam moves & reverse line movement signal sharp bettor consensus. Follow sharp, not public.
Injury Report
QB > OL > WR1 > RB in positional value hierarchy. Wednesday–Friday report changes move lines.
Bye Week Bounce
Post-bye teams historically +1.5 pts better ATS. Rest disadvantage teams go under more often.
Referee Tendencies
Flags/game, PI rate, holding rate — high-flag refs favor totals going over.
Division Game Context
Division rivals beat the spread at higher rates — familiarity narrows talent gap.

🎓 NCAAF-Specific Key Factors

SP+ / FPI Ratings
Bill Connelly's SP+ is the gold standard. More predictive than W-L record for ATS.
Recruiting Rankings
247Sports composite — massive talent gap in G5 vs P4 matchups. Regresses season-to-season.
Transfer Portal Net Rating
Portal gains/losses relative to returning production %. High impact on early-season lines.
Returning Production %
% of yards/tackles returning from prior year. High returning teams less volatile early.
Rivalry / Emotion Factor
Rivalry games compress expected margins — talent gaps matter less than in neutral games.
Travel Distance
Cross-country travel (3+ time zones) impacts G5 road teams more than P4.
Conference SOS
P4 non-con scheduling inflates records — adjust SP+ for conference strength.
Coaching ATS History
Some coaches (Saban-era, Dabo) dramatically beat market expectations. Track coach ATS.

🎯 Player Props Key Factors

Target Share (>25% = elite)
Single most predictive receiving metric. Public lines often lag role changes by 2–3 weeks.
Air Yards / aDOT
Average depth of target reveals route tree and TD equity beyond raw receptions.
Snap Count / Route %
100% routes run + high snap % = volume floor. Committee backs have lower floors.
CB Shadow Coverage
Elite CB following WR1 suppresses targets regardless of receiver talent.
Game Script (Spread)
Projected blowout = more rush attempts for winning RB, fewer targets for trailing WRs.
Vegas Total (Pass/Rush Split)
High O/U = more volume for all skill positions. Low O/U = under on most prop lines.
Recent Form (L3–L4)
More actionable than season averages — catches role changes and system adjustments early.
Red Zone Usage
RZ targets & carries = TD equity. Tracks scoring opportunity regardless of total yardage.

📖 Model Methodology

Phase 1 — Data Collection
Pull historical game data (2015–present) from nflfastR, CFBD API, and The Odds API. Merge betting lines, weather data, injury reports, and team efficiency metrics into a unified game-level dataset.

Phase 2 — Feature Engineering
Compute rolling averages (L3, L5, season) for EPA/play, yards/play, turnover margin, and pace. Calculate home-field values by stadium/conference. Flag weather games (>15 mph wind, <32°F, precipitation).

Phase 3 — Model Training
Train separate models for spread, totals, and moneyline using gradient boosting (XGBoost). Validate on holdout seasons. Target 55–58% ATS accuracy for spread model (break-even ~52.4% at -110 juice). Total model targets 54–56%.

Phase 4 — Line Comparison & Edge Detection
Compare model's implied spread to opening Vegas line. Edge = |model_spread − vegas_line|. Bet only when edge ≥ 2.0 pts (spread) or ≥ 1.5 pts (totals). Track CLV (closing line value) as model quality metric.

Phase 5 — Props Pipeline
Scrape projected usage (PFF/EVAnalytics), match to defensive grades, apply game script projection. Compare model projection to book line. Props edge threshold: ≥ 10% probability advantage vs. implied book odds.

Kelly Criterion Sizing
Bet size = (edge × odds) / (odds² − 1) × bankroll. Use half-Kelly or quarter-Kelly to reduce variance. Never bet more than 5% of bankroll on a single game.

Recommended Stack
Python (pandas, sklearn, xgboost) · nflfastR via nfl4th · CFBD Python client · Jupyter for EDA · SQLite for local storage · This dashboard for visualization.
